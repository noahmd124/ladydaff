About Us

Lady Daffodil is an 100% ECO, UK based clothing brand. We strive to offer quality clothes, with fasionable and cool designs. Using a mix of online-curated designs, and hand-drawn designs from our very own co-founder, we truly understand the meaning of good clothes and artwork.

Why Lady Daffodil?

Why Lady Daffodil? We are climate friendly, UK based and all of our clothing is hand printed, packaged and sent in Bristol - while still keeping our prices relatively low.

We were founded this year by budding-artist and animal-lover Molly MD and tech-savvy Noah MD, who hand prints every t-shirt.
